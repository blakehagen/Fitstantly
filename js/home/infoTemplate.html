Welcome to Fitstantly

See your best. Beat your best. Every instant matters.

What is Fitstantly?

Fitstantly is a clean, simple web application that shows your Fitbit data for today and the last 7 days.

Fitstantly is designed to help you compete against yourself.

In the short-term.

Get a clear picture of where you are and go do better.

Why Fitstantly?

"Fitstantly" is a play on the words "fit" and "instantly." Use Fitstantly to instantly see your Fitbit data for the last 7 days.

It's up to you whether you choose to go out and beat your best, not only for the last 7 days but for all-time.

See your data. Then go do the work.
