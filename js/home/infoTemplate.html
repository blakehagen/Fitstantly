Welcome to Fitstantly

See your best. Beat your best. Every instant matters.

What is Fitstantly?

Fitstantly is designed to help you compete against yourself.

In the short-term.

Fitstantly is a clean, simple web application that shows your Fitbit data for today and the last 7 days.

Get a clear picture of where you are and work to set a personal best for the week or for all-time.

Why Fitstantly?

"Fitstantly" is a play on the word "instantly." Use Fitstantly to instantly see your stats for the last 7 days.

It's up to you whether you choose to work hard to beat your best, not only for the last 7 days but for all-time.
