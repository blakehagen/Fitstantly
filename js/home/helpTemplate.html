Fitstantly: What is it?

This is what Fitstantly 1.0 looks like when you log in. You will see your personal Fitbit data for the current day, along with your personal bests for steps and "active minutes" for the last 7 days. You'll also see a chart showing your 7-day-steps trend and your steps average.

Known Issues

Incorrect "All-Time Steps Record" Data

Some users have reported their "All-Time Steps Record" data is not correct. This is a Fitbit issue. Fitstantly is pulling the correct data from the Fitbit API, but the data from Fitbit may not be correct. Verify in your Fitbit profile page under "My Achievements" ---> "Best" and see what Fitbit shows as your 1-day steps record. If your data is not correct, contact Fitbit support and they will resolve the issue.
