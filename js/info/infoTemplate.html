Info

Fitstantly connects you to your last 7 days of Fitbit data using the Fitbit API. It is designed to help you compete against yourself. Fitstantly was inspired by my wife, Emily, who got tired of seeing her friends on Fitbit have thousands of more steps than she did. It demotivated her instead of motivating her because she was so far behind.

No matter if your average is 4,000 steps/day or 40,000 steps/day, you can push to do better and set your own personal bests. Don't worry about anyone else, just focus on you and your activity level.
